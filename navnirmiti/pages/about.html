about us
We believe quality education should be affordable for all
We are a non-profit organisation working towards universal quality education. Since 1995, we have had one aim: To create a new age of high quality, low cost/no cost learning. We design and develop innovative methods that make learning a joyful process.

We integrate hands-on activities with mathematics and elementary science which ignites curiosity, rational thinking and scientific temperament. Our curriculum starts early, with special focus on elementary math and science, with creative tools to help explain concepts.

As a Self-Reliant way of working, we believe in generating income and resources to support our activities and vision.
